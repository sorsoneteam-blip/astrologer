Thumb Analysis

Secrets of the Thumb

👍
First Phalange (Will)

The tip of your thumb reveals the strength of your willpower, determination, and leadership. A broad tip = decisive. Pointed = creative. Waist-shaped = diplomatic.

🧠
Second Phalange (Logic)

The middle section reveals reasoning ability. Long = excellent logic. Short = relies on intuition. Thick = practical. Thin = idealistic planner.

♥
Mount of Venus

The fleshy base of the thumb (mount of Venus) governs love, sensuality, vitality, and generosity. Firm and prominent = passionate nature.

📐
Thumb Angle

90° = generous and open. 45° = balanced temperament. 30° or less = reserved, cautious, careful with resources. Over 90° = highly extroverted.

🌊
Flexibility

A flexible thumb = adaptable, generous, and open-minded. Stiff = disciplined, cautious, fixed in views. The bend at the top joint tells the most about emotional flexibility.

🔮
Thumb Reading

In South Indian palmistry, the thumb alone can reveal a person's entire character, destiny, and fate with remarkable accuracy — each whorl, ridge, and line a cosmic signature.
